Welcome to Project Nutmeg! As a part of the course Human Factors Interface Design, our team is attempting to design a product for the primary food providers of their families. Though the idea for a product originally began as an app that could keep inventory of our user's kitchen, we are developing an app interface that enables users to record and document their cooking history.
