Welcome to Project Nutmeg

We are team Nutmeg of the course Human Factors Interface Design at Olin College of Engineering. Over the course of the fall semester, our team will attempt to design a software interface for people who are primarily responsible for providing food for their families. Our goal is to provide a way for them to track the food in their kitchen/fridge and, as a result, make their grocery shopping experience easier.
